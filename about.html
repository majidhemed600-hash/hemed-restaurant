About Us

Discover the story behind Majeed Restaurant

Our Story

Majeed Restaurant was founded with a simple goal: to share the authentic tastes of East Africa with the people of Arusha and visitors from around the world. Nestled at the foot of Mount Meru, our restaurant blends traditional Tanzanian recipes with a modern, welcoming atmosphere. Every dish tells a story passed down through generations of local cooks and farmers.

Our Mission

To serve delicious, authentic African meals made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients while providing outstanding hospitality to every guest who walks through our doors.

Our Vision

To become the leading modern African restaurant in Tanzania, known for celebrating local culture through food, service, and community.

Why Choose Us

What makes Majeed Restaurant special

  1. Fresh ingredients sourced from local farmers every day.
  2. Authentic recipes prepared by experienced Tanzanian chefs.
  3. A warm, modern dining atmosphere for family and friends.
  4. Friendly and attentive customer service.
  5. Affordable prices without compromising on quality.

Our Values

  • Quality: We never compromise on freshness or taste.
  • Community: We support local farmers and suppliers.
  • Respect: We treat every guest like family.
  • Consistency: We deliver the same great experience every visit.
